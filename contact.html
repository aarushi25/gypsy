Food Republic, Vivo City, Singapore

At Vivo City, go to Food Republic at the top to eat - a huge government-controlled hawker centre where it's fun to select different dishes from different stalls. Some of Singapore's famous dishes include Nasi Padang (rice with chicken curry), Char Kway Tow (yummy fried noodles with soya sauce) and Mee Goreng (Malaysian noodles).
